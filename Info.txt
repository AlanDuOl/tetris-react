Block rotations:
    - Central point is equal to tile 4 point and a fake point for Z and T

    - Position correction after rotate points    
        - L, J, S, Z and T:
            - 0º:
                = x - tileDim
                = y + tileDim
            - 90º:
                = x - tileDim
            - 180º:
                = y - tileDim * 2
            - 270º:
                = x + tileDim * 2
                = y + tileDim
        - I:
            - Rotate clockwise for 90º and 270º
            - Rotate anti-clockwise for 0º and 180º

    - Fake central point for Z and T:
        - 0º: p4.y + tileDim
        - 90º p4.x - tileDim
        - 180º: p4.y - tileDim
        - 270º: p4.x + tileDim
